# Design System - Based on Thiel Fellowship

## Color Palette
- `--color-background-dark`: #000000  
- `--color-background-light`: #FFFFFF  
- `--color-text-primary`: #FFFFFF  
- `--color-text-secondary`: #F5F5F5  
- `--color-accent`: #333333  

## Typography
- **Font Family:** 'Inter', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif.  
- **Heading sizes:**  
  - H1: 64px weight 700 (bold)  
  - H2: 48px weight 700  
  - H3: 32px weight 600  
  - Body: 18px weight 400  
  - Small: 14px weight 400  
- **Line Height:** 1.2 for headings, 1.6 for body text.  

## Layout & Spacing
- Use generous vertical spacing between sections (~80px) and horizontal padding around content (~32px).  
- Section backgrounds alternate between dark and light to create contrast.  
- Content is centered within a max-width container of 1200px.  

## Components
- **Buttons:**  
  - Primary button: dark background (#000000) with white text; uppercase; padding 12px 24px; border radius 4px.  
  - Secondary button: transparent background with 1px solid white border and white text; same padding & radius.  
- **Cards/Panels:** Use dark background (#000) with white text, or light background (#FFF) with dark text. Provide padding of 32px and border radius 8px.  

## Imagery & Icons
- Use simple, minimalistic icons in monochrome (white on dark backgrounds and dark on light backgrounds).  
- Imagery should maintain a consistent aspect ratio and should align with the modern, clean aesthetic.
